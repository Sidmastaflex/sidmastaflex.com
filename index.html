About

Sidmastaflex is a DJ, producer, event curator, and beatboxer from the Inland Empire making waves across Southern California. Known for heddy song selection, crowd-reading, and mixing genres like oil and water, he takes every audience on a fresh emotional journey — heavy basslines, deep grooves, and rhythms that hit just right.
